Welcome to the **AI Computer Vision** Validated Pattern workshop.

This lab guide walks you through a multi-cluster deployment that combines Red Hat OpenShift Container Platform (OCP), Red Hat OpenShift AI (RHOAI), Red Hat Developer Hub (RHDH), and edge inference with NeuroFace computer vision workloads.

== What you will explore

* Hub-spoke fleet management with Red Hat Advanced Cluster Management (RHACM)
* AI inference at the edge with OpenVINO Model Server (OVMS) and YOLO
* Cross-cluster connectivity with Skupper and Red Hat Connectivity Link (RHCL)
* Observability with Grafana, Kiali, and OpenTelemetry
* Developer self-service with Developer Hub, GitLab, and OpenShift DevSpaces

== Workshop access

Use your assigned workshop user (`user1` through `user30`) with password `Welcome123!` to log in to:

WARNING: These credentials are for this ephemeral sandbox environment only. Do not reuse workshop passwords in production. Sandbox clusters are automatically destroyed after the session.

* OCP Console (HTPasswd identity provider `workshop-users`)
* Red Hat Developer Hub (Keycloak realm `backstage`)
* GitLab and DevSpaces (provisioned per user)

If you have not registered yet, open `%REGISTRATION_URL%` and enter your email to receive a `userN` assignment.
